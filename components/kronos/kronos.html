Who Are We?

Created in 2020 during the rampant outbreak of COVID-19, the team's biggest obstacle was being able to come together and work together to create a robot. Although we weren't able to compete in our first year, we were able to connect with our community in many different ways such as workshops and fundraisers. In 2021, we've continued to reach out to our community and we've not only enabled the team to gain hands-on experience in building a robot but also how to work together and problem solve.
