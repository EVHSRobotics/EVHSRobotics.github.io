FTC - Artemis

Founded in the middle of Covid pandemic, we always consider ourselves as the challengers. We started hard with different obstacles from short in technical knowledge to lack of competition experience. Despite all those hardships, we still managed to have our robot built and competed in the Qualifying rounds. To give back to our community after all the help we received, we hosted lots of different summer and school-year workshops to spread STEM. This year, we set ambitious goals both in awards and outreaches to continue growing to a better team: Reach at least 200 kids in our community for our outreach events, Get 1st Place in at least one award
