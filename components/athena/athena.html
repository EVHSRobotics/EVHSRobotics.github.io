Who Are We

In 2015, our team started with only four girls who noticed the gender disparity in the school’s robotics club and the STEM field. Today, our team and the amount of girls in our club has grown significantly. Named after the greek goddess Athena for her strength and intelligence, our goal is to inspire and include the underrepresented in STEM, especially girls, to pursue these fields through FIRST! In addition to building an amazing robot and improving our technical skills, our goals are to: Build teamwork, time management, and problem solving abilities, Provide an introduction of STEM and FIRST to the community, Recruit and introduce members to the joys of robotics
